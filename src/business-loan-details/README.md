# Business Loan Details Widget

This widget displays comprehensive information about Bajaj Finserv Business Loans.

## Features

- Collateral-free business loans
- Interest rates starting from 12% p.a.
- Loan amount: ₹1 Lakh - ₹80 Lakh
- Tenure: 12 - 84 months
- Quick disbursal within 48 hours
- Key features including minimal documentation
- Eligibility criteria
- Required documents
- Direct application link
- Vertical image carousel with multiple product images

## Usage

This widget is specifically designed for business loan queries and will be called when users ask about:
- Business loans
- BL
- MSME loans
- Working capital loans
- Business funding
- Or any variation/misspelling related to business loans

The AI will intelligently route business loan queries to this dedicated widget.
